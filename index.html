The annual Cultural Fest at Greenfield High School was a vibrant and memorable event. Colorful decorations adorned the school grounds, while music and laughter echoed throughout the day. Students showcased their talents through dance, drama, and musical performances, captivating the audience. Food stalls offered a variety of delicious treats, adding to the festive atmosphere.
